Robocon 2014

Robocon 2014

This year the theme explored parenthood. Two robots had to be designed (depicting a child and a parent) which performed a variety of tasks on recreational equipment akin to a child playing on a playground. In order to complete the theme, the robots had to “play” on a seesaw, swing, pole-walk and jungle gym.

 

This year included one of the most complex robots built by our team, featuring a gantry system with 4 degrees of freedom built on the base holonomic drive. The second robot was equipped with one of most compact electronic control systems we have designed.

 

For more information, please visit our official team website http://robocon.in/
