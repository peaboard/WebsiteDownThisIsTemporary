ArduDIY

ArduDIY - The Do-It-Yourself Arduino Kit

Complete kit containing all material - components as well as instructions required to make an Arduino from scratch. Meant to get young engineers started into the world of embedded electronics and software.  Inspiration for building this was to bridge the gap in theory and practical as well as generate curiosity and interest into domains where young undergrad students fear to venture alone.

Since it's inception as a physical product it has transformed into more of a all inclusive guide to get started with Arduino through the hardware concept.
The documentation has been migrated in an easy to read format which is available here. Please feel free to contribute.
